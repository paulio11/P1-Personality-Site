Kyle Bosman

Hi I'm Kyle Bosman - Dark Prince of Nerd Media.

A failed stand-up comedian that now makes a YouTube series called Delayed Input, an editorial show about a weird person and his bad opinions about video games. I also stream on Twitch three nights a week. You might remember me from my time at Easy Allies where I hosted the Easy Allies podcast and helped produce other content. Before that I was a monkey working as part of the Gametrailers.com team. Please don't take anything I say seriously.

Delayed Input

My new YouTube series falls in line with a lot of what I've made in the past. A spiritual sequel to my original show The Final Bosman. It'll be bizarre and comedic and will usually have some sort of perspective in the end. My goal with this series in particular is to create something that reflects earth's current vibes as well as my own increasingly inconsistent sense of being. You can help support Delayed Input on my Patreon page. Each season will be 8 episodes followed by a short break. New episodesmost Thursdays.
